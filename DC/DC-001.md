# DC-001 — Cooling Failure

## Scenario
Data centre cooling system failure leading to thermal risk escalation.

---

## Trigger Conditions
- Cooling unit failure detected
- Temperature rising beyond safe threshold
- CRAC/Chiller alarm activated

---

## Indicators
- Rack temperature increase
- Cooling system telemetry alerts
- Power usage spike (compensatory load)
- Hardware thermal warnings

---

## Threshold Logic

### GREEN
Normal temperature and cooling stability

### YELLOW
Minor cooling inefficiency detected

### ORANGE
Sustained temperature rise, partial cooling failure

### RED
Cooling failure with immediate thermal risk to hardware

---

## Expected Cascade

Cooling failure  
→ Temperature rise  
→ Server throttling  
→ System performance degradation  
→ Potential hardware shutdown

---

## Contingency Actions
- Activate backup cooling systems
- Reduce compute workload
- Shift critical services
- Notify operations team
- Monitor thermal thresholds

---

## Recovery Actions
- Restore cooling systems
- Rebalance workloads
- Verify hardware integrity
- Resume normal operations
