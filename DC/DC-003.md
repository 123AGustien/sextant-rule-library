# DC-003 — Network Congestion

## Rule Information

Rule ID: DC-003

Domain: Data Centre Resilience

Category: Network Performance

Status: Active

Version: 1.0

Repository: Sextant Rule Library

---

## Purpose

Evaluate the resilience of a data centre during periods of network congestion and assess potential cascading effects across interconnected operational, cyber, infrastructure, and financial domains.

---

## Scenario

Network traffic exceeds normal operating capacity due to increased demand, equipment limitations, routing issues, or external events, resulting in degraded service performance and increased operational risk.

The scenario evaluates:

- Network utilisation
- Bandwidth availability
- Latency
- Packet loss
- Service response time
- Infrastructure capacity

---

## Primary Indicators

- Network bandwidth utilisation
- Network latency
- Packet loss
- Throughput
- Router utilisation
- Switch utilisation
- Error rates
- Service response time

---

## Risk Levels

### GREEN

Network operating within normal capacity.

Traffic flows efficiently with acceptable latency and no service degradation.

No contingency actions required.

---

### YELLOW

Early signs of network congestion.

Monitor:

- Bandwidth utilisation
- Latency
- Packet loss
- Router performance

Action:

Increase monitoring and optimise network traffic where appropriate.

---

### ORANGE

Significant network congestion.

Indicators:

- High bandwidth utilisation
- Increased latency
- Rising packet loss
- Reduced application performance

Potential Cascade:

Network Congestion

↓

Application Slowdown

↓

Service Degradation

↓

Operational Stress

Action:

Optimise traffic routing, prioritise critical services, and prepare additional network capacity if available.

---

### RED

Critical network congestion.

Indicators:

- Network saturation
- Severe packet loss
- Extended latency
- Service interruptions

Potential Cascade:

Network Congestion

↓

Network Failure

↓

Application Outage

↓

Operational Disruption

↓

Cross-Domain Systemic Risk

Action:

Activate emergency network response procedures, restore critical connectivity, and initiate disaster recovery where required.

---

## Cross-Domain Dependencies

Data Centre (DC)

↓

Infrastructure (INF)

↓

Cyber (CYB)

↓

Financial (FIN)

Network congestion may disrupt digital infrastructure, cybersecurity monitoring, operational services, and financial transactions.

---

## Contingency Actions

- Monitor network performance continuously
- Optimise routing and traffic prioritisation
- Increase available bandwidth where possible
- Protect critical business services
- Assess cascading operational impacts
- Escalate to incident management when thresholds are exceeded
