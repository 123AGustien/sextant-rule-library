# DC-005 — Blackout Recovery

## Rule Information

Rule ID: DC-005

Domain: Data Centre Resilience

Category: Total Power Loss & Recovery

Status: Active

Version: 1.0

Repository: Sextant Rule Library

---

## Purpose

Evaluate data centre resilience during complete power loss events and assess recovery behaviour, system restoration sequencing, and cascading impacts across dependent domains.

---

## Scenario

A full power outage occurs due to grid failure, infrastructure collapse, or upstream utility disruption. The data centre transitions into emergency backup power systems and recovery protocols are initiated.

The scenario evaluates:

- Power restoration time
- Generator reliability
- UPS bridging performance
- System restart sequencing
- Data integrity
- Service recovery stability

---

## Primary Indicators

- Total power status
- Generator activation time
- UPS discharge rate
- System reboot sequence status
- Storage integrity checks
- Service restoration progress
- Recovery time objective (RTO)
- Recovery point objective (RPO)

---

## Risk Levels

### GREEN

Normal power conditions or successful recovery completed.

All systems stable and fully operational.

No contingency actions required.

---

### YELLOW

Early recovery phase or minor instability.

Monitor:

- System restart sequencing
- Partial service restoration
- Generator performance

Action:

Continue controlled recovery and validate system integrity.

---

### ORANGE

Partial recovery with instability.

Indicators:

- Delayed system recovery
- Partial service outages
- Increased error rates
- Infrastructure stress during restart

Potential Cascade:

Power Loss Recovery

↓

Service Instability

↓

Data Synchronisation Delays

↓

Operational Degradation

Action:

Stabilise systems, prioritise critical services, and validate infrastructure integrity.

---

### RED

Critical recovery failure or extended blackout.

Indicators:

- Prolonged power outage
- Generator failure
- Failed system recovery
- Data corruption risk

Potential Cascade:

Blackout Event

↓

Infrastructure Collapse

↓

Data Loss Risk

↓

Service Outage

↓

Cross-Domain Systemic Risk

Action:

Activate disaster recovery protocols, initiate failover systems, and perform full integrity restoration.

---

## Cross-Domain Dependencies

Data Centre (DC)

↓

Infrastructure (INF)

↓

Cyber (CYB)

↓

Financial (FIN)

Blackout events may trigger cascading impacts across operational, cybersecurity, and financial systems.

---

## Contingency Actions

- Activate disaster recovery procedures
- Validate system integrity during recovery
- Prioritise critical service restoration
- Monitor generator and UPS performance
- Review recovery timelines (RTO/RPO)
- Escalate if recovery thresholds are exceeded
