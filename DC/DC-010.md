# DC-010 — Multi-System Cascade Failure

## Rule Information

Rule ID: DC-010

Domain: Data Centre Resilience

Category: System-Wide Failure Cascade

Status: Active

Version: 1.0

Repository: Sextant Rule Library

---

## Purpose

Evaluate data centre resilience under simultaneous or cascading failures across multiple subsystems including power, cooling, compute, network, and storage, and assess systemic collapse behaviour.

---

## Scenario

A primary failure event triggers secondary and tertiary failures across interconnected systems. This may originate from power instability, cooling failure, network outage, or storage degradation, propagating across infrastructure layers.

The scenario evaluates:

- Cross-system dependency failure
- Cascade propagation speed
- Redundancy effectiveness
- Recovery coordination complexity
- System-wide stability thresholds

---

## Primary Indicators

- Multi-domain failure alerts
- Simultaneous subsystem degradation
- Redundancy activation success rate
- Recovery coordination delays
- Infrastructure interdependency stress
- System availability collapse rate

---

## Risk Levels

### GREEN

All systems stable.

No cross-domain instability detected.

No contingency actions required.

---

### YELLOW

Minor multi-system stress indicators.

Monitor:

- Isolated subsystem degradation
- Early dependency strain
- Increased recovery coordination events

Action:

Increase system-wide monitoring and validate redundancy pathways.

---

### ORANGE

Significant cascading instability.

Indicators:

- Multiple subsystem degradation
- Partial redundancy failure
- Increased service instability
- Cross-system load imbalance

Potential Cascade:

Subsystem Failure

↓

Cross-Domain Stress

↓

Service Degradation

↓

Operational Instability

Action:

Stabilise affected systems, isolate failing components, and reinforce redundancy layers.

---

### RED

Critical multi-system cascade failure.

Indicators:

- Simultaneous subsystem failures
- Redundancy collapse
- System-wide instability
- Severe service outage

Potential Cascade:

Multi-System Failure

↓

Infrastructure Collapse

↓

Service Outage

↓

Data Loss Risk

↓

Cross-Domain Systemic Crisis

Action:

Activate full disaster recovery protocols, initiate emergency failover systems, and coordinate system-wide restoration efforts.

---

## Cross-Domain Dependencies

Data Centre (DC)

↓

Infrastructure (INF)

↓

Cyber (CYB)

↓

Financial (FIN)

Failure in one subsystem may amplify stress across all connected domains, increasing systemic risk exponentially.

---

## Contingency Actions

- Activate full-system monitoring
- Prioritise critical infrastructure restoration
- Isolate failing subsystems
- Engage disaster recovery protocols
- Coordinate cross-domain response teams
- Escalate immediately if cascading accelerates
