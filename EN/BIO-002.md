🛰 BIO-002
Biodiesel Shortage Scenario Resilience Rule
Rule Metadata
Rule ID: BIO-002
Version: 1.0
Domain: EN - Energy Resilience
Category: Biofuel Supply Disruption
System: Sextant Protocol Resilience Simulation Framework

Purpose
Define a governed resilience rule for evaluating severe biodiesel shortage scenarios and their potential impact on energy security, critical infrastructure continuity, logistics operations, and economic resilience.

Scenario
Biodiesel Shortage Event
A critical disruption affecting biodiesel availability caused by:

Severe CPO supply reduction
Biodiesel production failure
Import supply interruption
Extreme oil price volatility
Distribution network disruption
Emergency fuel demand increase
Input Indicators
1. Biodiesel Blend Ratio
Parameter: Blend Ratio (%)

Purpose:

Measures national biodiesel dependency and fuel integration strategy.

2. CPO Strategic Stock Availability
Parameter: CPO Stock (%)

Purpose:

Measures available feedstock buffer resilience.

3. Import Dependency
Parameter: Import Dependency (%)

Purpose:

Measures exposure to external fuel supply disruption.

4. Oil Price Condition
States:

NORMAL
HIGH
EXTREME
5. Supply Availability
States:

STABLE
REDUCED

CRITICAL SHORTAGE
Risk Threshold Logic
LOW RISK
Conditions:

CPO Stock > 70%
Import Dependency < 40%
Supply Availability STABLE
Oil Price NORMAL
Action:

Maintain monitoring and reserve planning.

MEDIUM RISK
Conditions:

CPO Stock 40-70%
Import Dependency 40-70%
Supply Availability REDUCED
Action:

Activate stabilization measures.

Required actions:

Increase inventory monitoring
Review consumption patterns
Prepare contingency allocation
HIGH RISK
Conditions:

CPO Stock < 40%
Import Dependency > 70%
Supply Availability SHORTAGE
Action:

Activate energy resilience response.

Required actions:

Prioritize critical infrastructure fuel supply
Activate strategic reserve management
Reduce non-essential consumption
CRITICAL RISK
Conditions:

CPO Stock < 20%
Import Dependency > 80%
Supply Availability CRITICAL SHORTAGE
Oil Price EXTREME
Action:

Activate emergency containment protocol.

Required actions:

Protect essential services
Deploy alternative energy sources
Coordinate supply recovery
Maintain human decision oversight
