# Purpose

Define a governed resilience rule for evaluating biodiesel supply stress scenarios and their potential impact on energy security, infrastructure operations, logistics continuity, and economic stability.


---

# Scenario

## Biodiesel Supply Stress Event

A disruption or instability affecting biodiesel availability caused by:

- CPO supply reduction
- Import dependency increase
- Oil price volatility
- Production limitation
- Distribution disruption


---

# Input Indicators

## 1. Biodiesel Blend Ratio

Parameter:
Blend Ratio (%)

Purpose:
Measures dependence on biodiesel integration strategy.


## 2. CPO Stock Availability

Parameter:
CPO Stock (%)

Purpose:
Measures available feedstock resilience.


## 3. Import Dependency

Parameter:
Import Dependency (%)

Purpose:
Measures exposure to external supply disruption.


## 4. Oil Price Condition

States:

- NORMAL
- HIGH
- EXTREME


---

# Risk Threshold Logic

## LOW RISK

Conditions:

- CPO Stock > 70%
- Import Dependency < 40%
- Oil Price NORMAL


Action:

Maintain monitoring status.


---

## MEDIUM RISK

Conditions:

- CPO Stock 40-70%
- Import Dependency 40-70%
- Oil Price HIGH


Action:

Activate stabilization measures.


---

## HIGH RISK

Conditions:

- CPO Stock < 40%
- Import Dependency > 70%
- Biodiesel shortage detected


Action:

Activate resilience response.

# Contingency Actions

- Monitor biodiesel inventory
- Assess supply availability
- Prioritize critical operations


## Stabilization Response

- Activate alternative sources


## Recovery Response

- Review supply chain resilience
- Update risk parameters
