# INF-005 — Data Centre Interconnect (DCI) Failure

## Rule Information

Rule ID: INF-005

Domain: Infrastructure Resilience

Category: Inter-Data Centre Connectivity Failure

Status: Active

Version: 1.0

Repository: Sextant Rule Library

---

## Purpose

Evaluate system resilience under failure of inter-data centre connectivity links, affecting replication, failover, and cross-region service continuity.

---

## Scenario

Dedicated high-speed links between data centres (DCI) experience degradation or complete failure due to fibre cuts, routing faults, hardware issues, or provider outages. This disrupts synchronisation between primary and secondary environments.

The scenario evaluates:

- Cross-region replication integrity
- Failover system readiness
- Latency between data centres
- Data consistency under split-brain risk
- Service continuity across regions

---

## Primary Indicators

- Inter-DC latency spikes
- Replication lag increase
- Packet loss on dedicated links
- Failover delay or failure
- Data inconsistency warnings
- Cross-region service desync events

---

## Risk Levels

### GREEN

DCI links stable and fully operational.

Replication and failover systems synchronized.

No contingency actions required.

---

### YELLOW

Minor interconnect instability detected.

Monitor:

- Increased replication delay
- Latency variation
- Link jitter

Action:

Validate replication health and monitor failover readiness.

---

### ORANGE

Significant DCI degradation.

Indicators:

- High replication lag
- Partial link failure
- Cross-region sync delays
- Reduced failover confidence

Potential Cascade:

DCI Instability

↓

Replication Delay

↓

Service Inconsistency

↓

Operational Impact

Action:

Throttle non-critical replication, stabilise routing, and prepare failover systems.

---

### RED

Critical interconnect failure.

Indicators:

- Loss of cross-region connectivity
- Replication breakdown
- Split-brain risk
- Failover system failure

Potential Cascade:

DCI Failure

↓

Data Divergence

↓

Service Outage

↓

Cross-Domain Systemic Risk

Action:

Isolate regions, enforce single-primary mode, and activate disaster recovery procedures.

---

## Cross-Domain Dependencies

Infrastructure (INF)

↓

↓

Cyber (CYB)

↓

DCI failure can directly impact data integrity, service availability, and financial transaction consistency.

---

## Contingency Actions

- Isolate affected data centre regions
- Enforce single-primary operation mode
- Restore interconnect links
- Validate replication integrity
- Monitor data consistency checks
- Escalate to network infrastructure team
