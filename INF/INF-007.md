# INF-007 — Load Balancer Failure

## Rule Information

Rule ID: INF-007

Domain: Infrastructure Resilience

Category: Traffic Distribution Failure

Status: Active

Version: 1.0

Repository: Sextant Rule Library

---

## Purpose

Evaluate system resilience under load balancer failure conditions where traffic distribution becomes uneven, leading to service overload, latency spikes, or partial outages.

---

## Scenario

A critical load balancing component fails due to misconfiguration, software bug, hardware fault, or health-check malfunction. Traffic routing becomes unstable or biased toward limited backend resources.

The scenario evaluates:

- Traffic distribution integrity
- Backend server overload risk
- Health-check accuracy
- Failover routing effectiveness
- Service latency stability

---

## Primary Indicators

- Uneven request distribution
- Backend CPU/memory spikes
- Increased response latency
- Health-check false positives/negatives
- Session routing inconsistencies
- Partial service outages
- Connection timeouts

---

## Risk Levels

### GREEN

Load balancers operating normally.

Traffic evenly distributed.

No contingency actions required.

---

### YELLOW

Early imbalance detected.

Monitor:

- Minor traffic skew
- Slight latency variation
- Health-check inconsistencies

Action:

Validate routing rules and backend health.

---

### ORANGE

Significant load distribution failure.

Indicators:

- Severe traffic skew
- Backend overload on select nodes
- Service degradation
- Partial endpoint failures

Potential Cascade:

Load Imbalance

↓

Server Overload

↓

Service Degradation

↓

Operational Impact

Action:

Rebalance traffic manually and isolate overloaded nodes.

---

### RED

Critical load balancing failure.

Indicators:

- Complete routing failure
- Backend collapse due to overload
- Service outage
- System-wide instability

Potential Cascade:

Load Balancer Failure

↓

Traffic Collapse

↓

Infrastructure Overload

↓

Cross-Domain Systemic Risk

Action:

Failover to backup load balancers and restore routing integrity immediately.

---

## Cross-Domain Dependencies

Infrastructure (INF)

↓

Data Centre (DC)

↓

Cyber (CYB)

↓

Financial (FIN)

Load balancing failures amplify instability across all dependent systems.

---

## Contingency Actions

- Switch to redundant load balancers
- Redistribute traffic manually
- Monitor backend health closely
- Validate routing configuration
- Isolate overloaded services
- Escalate to network operations team
