# INF-010 — Multi-Layer Infrastructure Collapse

## Rule Information

Rule ID: INF-010

Domain: Infrastructure Resilience

Category: System-Wide Infrastructure Failure

Status: Active

Version: 1.0

Repository: Sextant Rule Library

---

## Purpose

Evaluate system resilience under extreme multi-layer infrastructure collapse where multiple infrastructure components fail simultaneously or in rapid succession.

---

## Scenario

A combined failure across network, DNS, power, cloud regions, and edge infrastructure leads to a systemic collapse of connectivity, compute availability, and service accessibility.

The scenario evaluates:

- Multi-layer dependency failure
- System-wide redundancy breakdown
- Recovery coordination complexity
- Cross-region instability
- Total infrastructure resilience threshold

---

## Primary Indicators

- Simultaneous subsystem failures
- Complete service unavailability
- Multi-region outage events
- DNS, power, and network collapse
- Failed failover mechanisms
- Infrastructure recovery paralysis
- Cross-domain cascading alerts

---

## Risk Levels

### GREEN

All infrastructure layers stable.

No anomalies detected.

No contingency actions required.

---

### YELLOW

Minor multi-layer stress detected.

Monitor:

- Isolated subsystem degradation
- Early redundancy strain
- Increased recovery coordination events

Action:

Validate full-stack resilience and redundancy pathways.

---

### ORANGE

Major multi-layer instability.

Indicators:

- Multiple simultaneous subsystem failures
- Partial infrastructure collapse
- Failover delays or inefficiencies
- Cross-system instability

Potential Cascade:

Infrastructure Stress

↓

Multi-System Degradation

↓

Service Disruption

↓

Operational Instability

Action:

Stabilise critical infrastructure layers and reinforce redundancy systems.

---

### RED

Full infrastructure collapse.

Indicators:

- Complete system unavailability
- Multi-layer redundancy failure
- Regional/global service outage
- Loss of recovery coordination capability

Potential Cascade:

Infrastructure Collapse

↓

Service System Failure

↓

Cross-Domain Systemic Crisis

↓

Financial & Operational Breakdown

Action:

Activate full disaster recovery protocols, isolate surviving systems, and initiate full restoration procedures.

---

## Cross-Domain Dependencies

Infrastructure (INF)

↓

Data Centre (DC)

↓

Cyber (CYB)

↓

Financial (FIN)

This is the highest-level infrastructure failure state, impacting all connected domains.

---

## Contingency Actions

- Activate full disaster recovery mode
- Restore services from backup regions
- Prioritise critical infrastructure recovery
- Isolate failing subsystems
- Coordinate cross-domain incident response
- Escalate to highest operational authority
