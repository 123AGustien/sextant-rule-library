# CYB-003 — Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS)

## Rule Information

Rule ID: CYB-003

Domain: Cyber Resilience

Category: Network Attack / Availability Disruption

Status: Active

Version: 1.0

Repository: Sextant Rule Library

---

## Purpose

Evaluate system resilience under Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) conditions and assess cascading impacts across network infrastructure, service availability, and dependent domains.

---

## Scenario

A coordinated high-volume traffic attack overwhelms network resources, bandwidth capacity, or application endpoints, causing service degradation or complete unavailability of critical systems.

The scenario evaluates:

- Network saturation levels
- Service availability degradation
- Load balancing effectiveness
- Firewall and mitigation response
- Latency escalation patterns

---

## Primary Indicators

- Incoming traffic volume spikes
- Packet loss increase
- Latency escalation
- Server CPU overload due to requests
- Firewall rule activation
- CDN / mitigation service activation
- Service downtime duration

---

## Risk Levels

### GREEN

No abnormal traffic detected.

Network and services operate normally.

No contingency actions required.

---

### YELLOW

Early-stage traffic anomalies detected.

Monitor:

- Traffic spikes
- Latency increases
- Unusual request patterns

Action:

Enable enhanced monitoring and verify mitigation readiness.

---

### ORANGE

Active DDoS attack in progress.

Indicators:

- Significant traffic overload
- Partial service degradation
- Increased latency and packet loss
- Mitigation systems engaged

Potential Cascade:

Traffic Flood

↓

Network Congestion

↓

Service Degradation

↓

Partial Outage

Action:

Activate traffic filtering, rate limiting, and mitigation services.

---

### RED

Critical DDoS saturation event.

Indicators:

- Full service unavailability
- Network saturation
- Mitigation capacity exceeded
- Infrastructure strain

Potential Cascade:

DDoS Attack

↓

Service Collapse

↓

Infrastructure Overload

↓

Cross-Domain Systemic Risk

Action:

Engage full-scale mitigation, reroute traffic through scrubbing systems, and isolate affected endpoints.

---

## Cross-Domain Dependencies

Cyber (CYB)

↓

Data Centre (DC)

↓

Infrastructure (INF)

↓

Financial (FIN)

DDoS attacks may disrupt digital services, financial systems, and operational monitoring platforms.

---

## Contingency Actions

- Activate DDoS mitigation services
- Apply rate limiting and filtering rules
- Redirect traffic through CDN/scrubbing layers
- Monitor service health continuously
- Protect critical APIs and endpoints
- Escalate if mitigation capacity is exceeded
