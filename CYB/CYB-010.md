# CYB-010 — Multi-Vector Coordinated Cyber Attack

## Rule Information

Rule ID: CYB-010

Domain: Cyber Resilience

Category: Advanced Persistent Threat / Coordinated Attack

Status: Active

Version: 1.0

Repository: Sextant Rule Library

---

## Purpose

Evaluate system resilience under a coordinated multi-vector cyber attack combining multiple intrusion methods such as ransomware, DDoS, credential compromise, API abuse, and supply chain exploitation.

---

## Scenario

A sophisticated attacker or threat group launches a coordinated attack across multiple vectors simultaneously. This includes network saturation, credential exploitation, system infiltration, and dependency compromise.

The scenario evaluates:

- Multi-vector attack coordination
- Detection and response latency
- Cross-system propagation
- Defense layer resilience
- System-wide stability under compounded stress

---

## Primary Indicators

- Simultaneous attack patterns across domains
- Mixed intrusion signatures (DDoS + malware + credential abuse)
- Rapid system degradation across services
- Security tool overload or blind spots
- Multiple alert system triggers
- Cross-domain failure correlation

---

## Risk Levels

### GREEN

No coordinated attack activity detected.

Security systems operating normally.

No contingency actions required.

---

### YELLOW

Early indicators of coordinated probing activity.

Monitor:

- Multiple low-level attack signals
- Distributed scanning behavior
- Minor anomalies across systems

Action:

Increase monitoring across all security layers.

---

### ORANGE

Active multi-vector attack underway.

Indicators:

- Simultaneous intrusion attempts across systems
- Partial service degradation across domains
- Security control strain
- Credential and network abuse combined

Potential Cascade:

Coordinated Attack

↓

Multi-System Stress

↓

Service Degradation

↓

Operational Instability

Action:

Activate full defensive posture, isolate affected vectors, and prioritize critical system protection.

---

### RED

Critical coordinated cyber assault.

Indicators:

- Full-scale multi-domain compromise attempts
- System-wide service disruption
- Defensive system overload
- Severe cross-domain propagation

Potential Cascade:

Multi-Vector Attack

↓

Infrastructure Breakdown

↓

System-Wide Failure Risk

↓

Cross-Domain Systemic Crisis

Action:

Initiate emergency response across all cyber defense layers, isolate affected infrastructure, and activate disaster recovery protocols.

---

## Cross-Domain Dependencies

Cyber (CYB)

↓

Data Centre (DC)

↓

Infrastructure (INF)

↓

Financial (FIN)

Coordinated attacks can simultaneously destabilize all dependent systems, creating systemic risk amplification.

---

## Contingency Actions

- Activate full incident response mode
- Segment and isolate affected systems
- Deploy multi-layer defense countermeasures
- Monitor all attack vectors in real time
- Preserve forensic and telemetry data
- Escalate to highest security authority level
