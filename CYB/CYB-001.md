# CYB-001 — Ransomware Attack

## Rule Information

Rule ID: CYB-001

Domain: Cyber Resilience

Category: Malware / Ransomware Incident

Status: Active

Version: 1.0

Repository: Sextant Rule Library

---

## Purpose

Evaluate system resilience under a ransomware attack scenario, where critical systems are encrypted or rendered unavailable, and assess cascading impacts across infrastructure, data integrity, and dependent domains.

---

## Scenario

A ransomware attack infiltrates the system through phishing, vulnerability exploitation, or compromised credentials. Critical systems, databases, or infrastructure components become encrypted or locked, restricting access and disrupting operations.

The scenario evaluates:

- System encryption impact
- Service availability
- Data accessibility
- Incident response effectiveness
- Cross-domain disruption propagation

---

## Primary Indicators

- Number of affected systems
- Encryption activity detection rate
- Endpoint compromise rate
- Service downtime duration
- Backup integrity status
- Network anomaly detection
- Privilege escalation attempts

---

## Risk Levels

### GREEN

No active cyber threats detected.

Systems are secure and operating normally.

No contingency actions required.

---

### YELLOW

Early-stage suspicious activity detected.

Monitor:

- Unusual login attempts
- Minor endpoint anomalies
- Increased network scanning activity

Action:

Increase monitoring and verify security controls.

---

### ORANGE

Active ransomware intrusion detected.

Indicators:

- Partial system encryption
- Unauthorized access confirmed
- Service degradation
- Lateral movement detected

Potential Cascade:

Cyber Intrusion

↓

System Encryption

↓

Service Disruption

↓

Operational Impact

Action:

Isolate affected systems, activate incident response, and protect backup infrastructure.

---

### RED

Critical ransomware outbreak.

Indicators:

- Widespread system encryption
- Backup compromise risk
- Major service outage
- Loss of operational control

Potential Cascade:

Cyber Attack

↓

System-Wide Lockdown

↓

Infrastructure Disruption

↓

Data Loss Risk

↓

Cross-Domain Systemic Crisis

Action:

Activate full disaster recovery, isolate network segments, and restore from secure backups.

---

## Cross-Domain Dependencies

Cyber (CYB)

↓

Data Centre (DC)

↓

Infrastructure (INF)

↓

Financial (FIN)

Cyber incidents may propagate into operational downtime, financial disruption, and infrastructure instability.

---

## Contingency Actions

- Isolate infected systems immediately
- Disable lateral movement paths
- Activate backup restoration protocols
- Monitor network traffic for persistence threats
- Escalate to incident response team
- Preserve forensic evidence for analysis
