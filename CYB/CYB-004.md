# CYB-004 — Insider Threat Event

## Rule Information

Rule ID: CYB-004

Domain: Cyber Resilience

Category: Internal Threat / Privileged Misuse

Status: Active

Version: 1.0

Repository: Sextant Rule Library

---

## Purpose

Evaluate system resilience under insider threat conditions, where a trusted user or privileged account misuses access to disrupt systems, exfiltrate data, or degrade operational integrity.

---

## Scenario

A user with legitimate access privileges intentionally or unintentionally causes harm through unauthorized actions, including data manipulation, deletion, leakage, or system disruption.

The scenario evaluates:

- Privileged access control effectiveness
- Audit trail integrity
- Data modification detection
- Lateral movement potential
- Incident response speed

---

## Primary Indicators

- Unusual privileged account activity
- Off-hours system access
- Bulk data downloads or deletions
- Configuration changes without approval
- Access pattern anomalies
- Privilege escalation attempts
- Audit log irregularities

---

## Risk Levels

### GREEN

No abnormal internal activity detected.

All access aligns with approved roles and policies.

No contingency actions required.

---

### YELLOW

Suspicious internal behavior detected.

Monitor:

- Unusual login times
- Elevated access requests
- Minor policy deviations

Action:

Increase auditing and verify privileged account activity.

---

### ORANGE

Confirmed or high-risk insider threat activity.

Indicators:

- Unauthorized data access
- System configuration changes
- Potential data leakage
- Privilege misuse detected

Potential Cascade:

Insider Action

↓

Data Manipulation / Exfiltration

↓

System Integrity Degradation

↓

Operational Disruption

Action:

Revoke or restrict access immediately and initiate internal incident response procedures.

---

### RED

Severe insider compromise event.

Indicators:

- Large-scale data theft or deletion
- Critical system sabotage
- Multiple privileged accounts compromised
- Audit system tampering

Potential Cascade:

Insider Breach

↓

System Sabotage

↓

Infrastructure Instability

↓

Financial & Operational Impact

↓

Cross-Domain Systemic Risk

Action:

Initiate emergency containment, lock affected accounts, preserve forensic evidence, and restore systems from secure backups.

---

## Cross-Domain Dependencies

Cyber (CYB)

↓

Data Centre (DC)

↓

Infrastructure (INF)

Insider threats may propagate into infrastructure disruption and financial system instability.

---

## Contingency Actions

- Immediately revoke suspicious access
- Isolate affected accounts and systems
- Preserve and secure audit logs
- Conduct forensic investigation
- Enforce credential rotation
- Escalate to security governance team
